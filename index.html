Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about Sword and Supper

Read Full FAQ on Reddit

Where are the old posts?

As mentioned in the Welcome Post, during the Closed Beta it's sometimes necessary for us to remove or reset content. In some cases, old data is not longer compatible with these new systems, and we have to remove it to keep the game working smoothly. We know no-one likes resets, so we avoid it wherever possible. We really appreciate your patience while we make these changes!

Character Stats

Primary Stats: Hitpoints (HP), Attack (ATK), and Defence (DEF) can be permanently increased by spending coins.

Secondary Stats: Speed, Dodge, and Crit cannot be permanently increased through coins but can be enhanced via equipment or map bonuses.

Abilities System

Abilities are special effects that trigger during combat. They can be obtained through:

  • Vending machines found on the map
  • Equipping specific items
  • Temple blessings

Each ability has specific triggers and effects that can significantly impact combat strategy.

Equipment and Crafting

Equipment provides various bonuses and abilities:

  • Rings: Often provide critical hit bonuses and special abilities
  • Armor: Provides defense, resistances, and shield abilities
  • Weapons: Primary attack power and special combat abilities

Many items can be crafted using blueprints and resources like Ore and Wood.

Mission Locations

Missions take place in various locations including:

  • Fields: Open areas with standard enemies
  • Forbidden City: More challenging areas with stronger foes
  • Mossy Forest: Natural environments with unique challenges

Each location offers different enemy types, loot, and difficulty levels.

My hearts didn't refresh! What can I do?

Try fully refreshing the page and reloading the app - there is an intermittent issue where your hearts have successfully recharged, but the display in-game doesn't update. A refresh should fix it.

What do the stats mean?

  • ATK: Raw attack power
  • DEF: Defense. Reduces incoming damage
  • SPD: Speed. Faster characters go first and can attack more than once before slower characters are ready
  • DGE: Dodge chance. Dodge an incoming attack for 0 damage.
  • CRT: Crit chance. Critical attacks do 2x damage.

How do I level up?

Use the Stats button (bottom left) to access the stat menu. The gold you earn in missions can be spent on upgrading your stats and boosting your level.

Why can I only level up HP, ATK and DEF?

The gold-based level-up system is designed to support upgrades to the core HP, ATK and DEF. Now that we've added more equipment with boosts for the secondary stats (like Speed and Crit), we've removed the ability to upgrade these directly.

How do I get equipment?

Beat missions to collect the treasure at the end - some missions reward with equipment as well as gold. Bosses also drop equipment loot. Check out the 'Equip' button (the little bag) to view your inventory and equip your character.

What's the deal with equipment marked as 'Beta'

During the early stages of the Beta, players were still receiving test equipment that isn't intended for inclusion in the full game. That equipment has now been retired and can no longer be obtained. If you already own some of this OG equipment, you can upgrade it to a new item and continue to use it.

What do the abilities do?

Abilities you gain from Temples are temporary and apply only to that Mission. Abilities granted by Equipment are active whenever that item is equipped.

  • Add Rage On Heal: Add rage whenever you heal.
  • Boost Attack On High HP: Boosts attack when HP is 100%.
  • Critical Recovery: Heal for 3% of Max HP whenever you land a critical hit.
  • Dodge if Low: Increases dodge chance by 20% when HP is below 30%.
  • Heal on Bolt: Heal a small amount whenever a lightning bolt fires.
  • Lightning Bolt: Zap your target with a lightning bolt at the start of your turn.
  • Lightning On Attack: When you attack, zap your target with a lightning bolt.
  • Lightning On Crit: When you make a critical attack, zap your target with a lightning bolt.
  • Magic Knife: Throw a magic knife at the start of your turn.
  • Magic Knife on Crit: Throw a magic knife whenever you make a critical attack.
  • Magic Knife On Rage: On Rage activation, throw a magic knife.
  • Second Wind: Heal for 10% of Max HP at the start of each of your next 3 turns the first time you dip below 30% HP.
  • Strike Twice Every Other: Every other turn, attack twice with your main weapon.

What's the yellow bar under my character?

Fighting is a hungry business! Every time you attack, your hunger meter will fill. When it's full, you'll unleash an extra-ferocious rage attack, and the bar will reset.

How can I create a Mission?

When you beat a Mission, you'll often receive a 'Map' item as part of the Mission reward. Open the Maps tab in your inventory and select a Map to use it.

Mission creators receive a bonus for playing their own mission - choose between extra gold, or stronger attack power. Mission creators also have the option to customize missions they create: play your own mission to add an enemy taunt that all other players will see.

How do I leave feedback?

Head over to the main discussion thread and add a comment.

Why was I invited?

Reddit sent out invites to the closed beta to active members of related gaming subreddits. Thanks for coming to check the game out!

Why is the subreddit called CabbageIdle?

CabbageIdle was the internal codename for the game before it became Sword & Supper.
